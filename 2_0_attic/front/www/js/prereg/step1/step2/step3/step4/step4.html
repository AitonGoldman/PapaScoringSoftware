Step 4 : Upload Player Picture
In this step, you must upload a picture of yourself.  It must be a picture of your face from the neck up. 

This will be used by scorekeepers and deskworkers to identify you.

Please use the "Choose Files" button to select a photo (or on a phone, take a picture), and then click the "Upload" button.





The picture displayed above is what will be used. If you like this picture, please click the "Next" button. If you do not like this picture, please select and upload another picture.
