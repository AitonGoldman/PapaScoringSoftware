IFPA Rank Lookup

Q. IFPA found multiple players, but none of them are me - what do I do?

A. Please make sure you enter your name as it appears on the IFPA site. If you do not have an IFPA ranking, please email prereg@papa.org

Q. I enetered my full name and IFPA still found too many players. What do I do?

A. Please email prereg@papa.org with your name and description of what has happened.

Q. IFPA says I do not have a ranking, but I know I do. What do I do?

A. Please check that you are entering your name as it appears on the IFPA website. If you are, please email prereg@papa.org

Q. IFPA is not reporting my ranking correctly. What do I do?

A. Please email prereg@papa.org with details of what IFPA is reporting.
